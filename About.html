About Us

We aim for the Art of French Perfumery, Reimagined for You.

Bottled Dreams and Wistful Memories.

At Preal perfumes, we believe a scent is more than a fragrance—it's a portal to a feeling. It’s the blush of a first kiss, the warmth of golden hour, the quiet peace of a morning all to yourself. Our perfumes are crafted to be your most intimate keepsakes, capturing the moments that define you. Founded by a lifelong dreamer with a passion for botany and nostalgia, each bottle holds a story. From the dewy, petal-soft embrace of our rose-centric fragrances to the sparkling, joyful burst of our citrus blends, we invite you to find the scent that tells your story.

Where fragrance is an extension of your identity. We believe every woman is multifaceted, and her scent should be too. Gone are the days of being defined by a single note. Our collection is designed for the woman who wears many hats—the CEO, the creative, the partner, the friend—and deserves a scent that evolves with her. We craft sophisticated, genderless-leaning fragrances with high-quality ingredients that are vegan, cruelty-free, and ethically sourced. Our scents are not about attracting a gaze, but about claiming your space. This is fragrance for the woman who knows that her most powerful accessory is her presence.

The Ingredient-Obsessed & Transparent Brand

What you put on your skin is as important as what you put in your body. preal is built on a foundation of radical transparency and an uncompromising commitment to quality. We believe you have a right to know where your scent comes from. We forge direct relationships with farmers and co-ops around the world, ensuring our ingredients are not only of the highest grade but also sourced ethically and sustainably. From our organic Bulgarian rose to our sustainably harvested Indonesian sandalwood, every component is chosen for its purity and potency. The result is a fragrance that is both beautiful and responsible.
